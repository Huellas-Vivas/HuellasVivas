# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We want Huellas Vivas to be a welcoming and respectful space for everyone.  
All contributors, maintainers, and participants are expected to follow this code of conduct.

## Expected Behavior

Please:

- Be respectful and inclusive.
- Use constructive language.
- Be patient with other contributors.
- Focus on improving the project.
- Accept feedback gracefully.

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behavior will not be tolerated:

- Harassment or discrimination of any kind
- Offensive or aggressive comments
- Personal attacks
- Trolling or intentionally disruptive behavior

## Enforcement

Project maintainers have the right to remove comments, commits, or contributions that violate this code of conduct.

Serious or repeated violations may result in a temporary or permanent ban from contributing.

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please open an issue or contact the maintainers.

We take all reports seriously and will respond as quickly as possible.

## Final Note

Let’s build a respectful and supportive community together.
